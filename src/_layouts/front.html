MapTop is mapping software for designers.

Ever wanted to incorporate a map into your design but got frustrated by the complicated software used by GIS professionals? Me too. That's why we're building MapTop.

With MapTop, you'll be able to easily create and style maps for use in your work.

Import Data

Quickly import data from Open Street Map, a community driven project to make map data freely available to all. From Countries and States, to Back-Roads and Lakes, anything you want to put on a map is at your fingertips.

Styling and Profiling

With MapTop, you can easily customize your maps, adjusting color themes and tweaking map attributes. If you've ever used a graphics editing program, you'll be right at home here.

Export Your Map

Once your map is complete, you'll want to be able to export it out of MapTop for use in your websites or other documents. We save our data out as standard SVG files, so you can take your design further however you want.
